Proposition: HMW create trustworthy AI based experiences that are relevant to our passenger vehicle users and our commercial vehicle users?

Abstract

People don't seem to be aware of AI in their daily lives even though AI is with them in the present era. Artificial intelligence technology is increasingly infiltrating our lives, but many people still tend to trust themselves more without believing in AI. However, AI can improve their lives in that not only efficiency and effectiveness, but also invisible quality of life becomes true friends with them.

This research began with the subtle human emotions that have a significant impact on people's daily lives. By supporting emotionally as a caring friend, improvised trip planner, and sharers with other drivers, we want drivers to appreciate their driving experience, feel more comfortable to use AI and trust AI than in the past.
